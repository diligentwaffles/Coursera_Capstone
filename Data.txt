DATA
Data source will be from FourSquare explore API and Google Places API. It will consist of the following features:
	• Will be focused on Sacramento
	• Location data to show quantity of salons/barbershops
		○ For Comparison, the quantity of business will be expressed as "per capita". To show the concentration of salons/babershops to show which cities are in need of a salon/babershop or are over-saturated
	• Services available
		○ This information will be used to inform potential businesses of what services of the market are needed in this city or to show potential customers what service(s) that interest them.  
	• Average price 
		○ This can show potential businesses the current market price to under-cut the market to stay competitive and build a client base. This will inform potential customers of potential savings by traveling to a nearby city.
	• Average ratings
		○ This will show potential businesses that it has bad reviews that they might be able to provide services with better customer service to capture this potential market.
